Rakim UP: about/Identity & Values

My personal top five things I value are family, friends, sleep, food, excercise and health (spiritually, mentally and physically).

Explain a situation where you've had an ethical desiion to make

Just at the end of the term 2/2022 at one of the basketball clubs I coach at we had an end of the term "tournament week". This usually means for our whole session kids will start with a quick 10 minute warm up, then get split into teams to play against eachother for the rest of the session.

There were 4 coaches present and we each had about 8 kids each during our high school (Year 9-13) session. This particular team that I was given only had about 2 upperclassmen (Year 12-13) whilst the other 3 teams bolstered about 4ish each. So off the bat I was put in a situation 1) I play the older more developed kids every game the whole game or 2) I rotate the beginners on regularly even if it means we fail to get off the bottom court and win a game?

I put being a fair, inclusive coach above being a winning coach and rotated all kids into the game only pulling them off when they weren't putting out their best. Basically all kids could stay on the court as long as they were giving there best effort on Offense AND Defense. This ensured not only ample play time for all my younger kids but more importantly it cemented the idea of effort above competency. Also, just as importantly it gave my older kids a chance to be put into leadership positions amongst their peers by 1)being better communicators and 2) leading by example.

In the end we won our last game out of the 6 we had and I couldnt be more prouder of my boys and girls.

Describe how your culture has influenced your values and Identity

I have two sides of family who are polar opposites in some traits but being Maori/Samoan the underlying values of family(mainly) are roughly about the same.

On one side of my family all of them have some kind of artistic or academic inclination: Musicians, Painters, Play Writes, Directors, Producers, Proffessors. While on the other side, all of them are more inclined practically: Makers, Tradesmen, Farmers of all sorts, Hunters, Gatherers, Athletes. I also strongly believe that theyre both extreme cases in both theyre respective feilds.

This kind of duality has heavily influenced my Identity growing up as to where I am at ease and confident in most environments doing most things. I may not be the best right away, but with a well rounded character, humility and time I know there will be countless iterations of improvement to look forward to.

I value my up fortunate upbringing, and I am grateful for the person I am because of them.
