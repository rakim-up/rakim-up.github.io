Discuss a time where you were blocked on a simple problem, and a time where you elegantly solved a problem

This weeks problem was that I got stuck motivating myself to actually work on things, and prioritizing in general. I just started reading something a new series I enjoy, and when I do start reading something captivating, I find it really easy to lose myself in whatever im reading at the time.

Although, it means I am now crunching things at the last minute once more, I dont feel too overwhelmed because I have had a list of things I have to finish day by day and it has held me accountable to the deadline so far (...so good...hopefully). Reflecting on this made me realise that it I put things off and do other things if im not too sure on the next step, and instead of reading for a few days and letting the content digest in the back of my head, I need to be more active with the implementation of the knowledge im trying to understand. In short it needs to turn from an abstract, philisophical thought to a "Oh, lemme muck around with it and see what happens"
