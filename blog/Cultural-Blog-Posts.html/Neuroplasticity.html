Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people.

Understainding the principles of neuroplasticity has many benefits. The basis of the principles are like the idea of building rapport. When an action is practiced or a memory accessed repeatedly the neural networks that house them are then made stronger which then make the action or memory that much more effective and easy to access/display. In short you can adapt both structure and function of your brain through repitition and experience.

Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your benefit.

I have always had a hard time with being a morning person. The idea of neuroplasticity has made me believe that waking up early and being a morning person can be obtained, as long as I stay connsistent and keep those neural networks firing day in day out. Also with coding I plan to implement this idea with the harder to grasp concepts and wrestle with them untill they get easier.

What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?

  • Memory tasks and games
  • Learning to juggle
  • Learning to play a new instrument
  • Learning anew language
  • Yoga
  • Mild to moderate excercise
  • Crosswords and Sudoku
  • Learning a new subject, especially large and complex ones in a short period of time
